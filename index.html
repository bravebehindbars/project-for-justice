About Us

Our Mission:To Empower Indigent Incarcerated Defendants Ensure Equal Access to Justice and a Successful Re-Entry Back into Society

We aim to:

  • Improve access to courts and legal research resources for incarcerated defendants
  • Ensure incarcerated defendants access to competent legal representation and a fair trial
  • Inform incarcerated defendants about education and employment opportunities during their confinement and after their release into the community
  • Advocate for indigent defense reforms
